Budjot: a simple budgeting tool to help you jot down your finances!

  • Simple, uncomplicated, everyday budgeting;
  • Keep track of all the bills and expenses you have throughout the month;
  • Understand what you've paid and what you haven't, so that you don't overspend.
  • Create a budget for every month;
  • Copy previous months;
  • See all your budgets in one place.
  • Create new budgets by entering your income;
  • Keep track of what bills you need to pay.
  • See your total expenditure;
  • Mark bills as paid;
  • See how much should be left in your bank account.
